Services

Specialties

I provide individual, couples and child counseling. Treatment specialization includes:

  • Depression, Anxiety and Trauma
  • Post Traumatic Stress Disorders (PTSD)
  • Mental and Physical Chronic Pain Issues
  • Couples Counseling and Parenting Support
  • Family and Difficult Children Issues
  • Military Family and Veterans Concerns
  • Workplace and Life Stress Management
  • Substance Abuse, Addiction and Recovery
  • Domestic Violence and Trauma Survivors
  • Adult Survivors of Childhood Abuse
  • Spiritual and Religious Concerns

Personalized Therapy

I work with a wide range of emotional, behavioral, and growth-oriented issues. In a comfortable and supportive atmosphere, I offer a highly personalized approach to help you attain the personal freedom you’re striving for.

You don't need a different therapy for every problem. If you are getting medically ill a lot and then you boost your immune system, you tend to be healthier across the board. Similarly, in psychology, if you are stressed and distressed, you can free up stuckpoints in the functioning of your mind/brain and in how you body/nervous system regulates you. And you are healthier, mentally and physically, across most areas of you life.

Healing Systems

I have dedicated myself to the knowledge and practice of many different systems of healing. Some of these which may be thought to be conflicting can co-exist when put together properly. I have the wise mind and experience to do this for you. I draw from the following:

  • Eye Movement Desensitization Reprocessing (EMDR)
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Guided Imagery, Somatic Experiencing (SE)
  • Solution Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT)
  • Internal Family Couples Systems (IFS)
  • Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy (MBCT)
  • Interpersonal Neurobiology (Daniel Siege)
  • Heart Rate Variability Biofeedback (BCIA-EEG)
  • Transpersonal (Spirtual) Psychotherapy

On-Line Counseling

Once I have gotten to know you and it's hard to meet me at my office, we can meet on-line. Sometimes you're too busy, live too far away, or have trouble find childcare. So, I've added a telemedicine, video counseling service. It is 100% HIPAA compliant, secure and confidential. It is easy to use. All you need is a computer and broadband internet access. Although I prefer to meet face-to-face, research has shown that internet therapy is effective. If you have any questions just let me know. I'm happy to help you figure out whether video counseling makes senses for the kind of work we are doing.
