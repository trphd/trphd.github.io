About

Background, Experience and Expertise

With over 35 years experience as a psychologist and child/family counselor, I currently serve in private practice working with a broad spectrum of clients. Among my areas of expertise are trauma/PTSD work, experiential (mind/body) EMDR, and child psychotherapy.

I have always been a full-time, front-line clinician and have experience with complex mental disorders. I have been in individual or group private practices in Honolulu, Kona, Reno, Annapolis, and Vancouver, BC. I have worked in medical facilities (Renown Health, Reno and Sierra Nevada Family Health, Carson City).

Education, License and Training

  • High School: Encina High School, Sacramento, CA (Freshman @ Marysville Union High School; Sophomore @ Chico Senior High School)
  • Undergraduate: University of California at Berkeley, 4 years-BA, Chemistry and Philosophy.
  • Graduate: University of Hawaii, Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology.
  • Internship (13 months of 40 hour a week supervised training!): Tripler Army Medical Center, Adolescent/Child Psychiatry Service, Honolulu.
  • California Licensed Psychologist # 22605
  • Registrant with the National Register for Health Care Providers in Psychology (National Register)
  • Certificate of Professional Qualification by the Association of State and Provincial Psychology Boards (ASPPB)
  • U.S. Army veteran.
